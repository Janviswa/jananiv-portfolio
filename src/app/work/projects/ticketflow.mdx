## Overview

TicketFlow is an AI-powered IT support platform that automates ticket classification, risk assessment, team assignment, and support assistance using Large Language Models (LLMs), reducing manual triage effort and improving resolution efficiency.

## Problem

IT support teams handle large volumes of tickets across multiple departments, making manual categorization, prioritization, and routing time-consuming and inconsistent. Delays in ticket assignment can increase resolution times and impact business operations.

## Solution

Built a full-stack AI-powered ticket management system that analyzes incoming tickets, automatically identifies the appropriate department, predicts risk level and priority, assigns tickets to support agents, and provides context-aware assistance through a Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) pipeline.

## Key Features

- **AI Ticket Classification**: Automatically categorizes tickets into departments such as IT, Security, Finance, HR, and Network.
- **Risk & Priority Assessment**: Uses AI-driven analysis to determine ticket severity and business impact.
- **Smart Ticket Assignment**: Automatically routes tickets to the most suitable support team or agent.
- **AI Support Assistant**: Provides context-aware responses using a RAG pipeline powered by ChromaDB.
- **Ticket Summarization**: Generates concise summaries for faster issue understanding.
- **Role-Based Dashboard**: Separate interfaces for Employees, Support Agents, and Administrators.
- **Knowledge Base Search**: Retrieves relevant solutions from stored support documentation.

## Technologies Used

- **Next.js & TypeScript** – Frontend development
- **FastAPI** – Backend REST APIs
- **PostgreSQL** – Ticket and user management
- **Llama 4 Scout (Groq)** – Ticket analysis and reasoning
- **DistilBERT** – Ticket classification and NLP processing
- **ChromaDB** – Vector database for RAG
- **Sentence Transformers** – Embedding generation
- **Docker** – Containerized deployment

## Challenges

Designing reliable AI workflows for ticket classification, risk scoring, and department routing required extensive prompt engineering and validation. Ensuring accurate retrieval from the knowledge base while minimizing irrelevant results was critical for improving response quality.

## Results

Automated ticket triage, risk assessment, and routing for support requests, reducing manual effort and enabling faster issue resolution through AI-assisted workflows.

## Key Learnings

Combining LLM-based reasoning with RAG significantly improves the accuracy and reliability of support responses. Structured AI outputs and retrieval grounding are essential for building production-ready enterprise AI applications.
